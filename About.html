Redefining Supply Chain Security

At Socket, we're transforming how organizations protect their applications from the rising threat of software supply chain attacks. Founded in 2021, Socket offers a developer-first platform that proactively detects and blocks malicious packages in real time, securing the open source ecosystems that power modern software.

With over 96% of today's codebases coming from open source, traditional security tools fall shortâ€”they're reactive and disrupt developer workflows. Socket takes a different approach: we prevent supply chain attacks before they happen, maintaining development velocity while mitigating emerging threats.

Socket safeguards all businesses including technology, media, healthcare, and financial services over 500 new-day attacks every week, ensuring that critical applications stay secure, fast, and developer-friendly.
